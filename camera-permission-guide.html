📱 Camera Permission Setup Guide

🎯 Goal: One-Time Camera Permission

Set up your browser to allow camera access permanently for Tindahan Ko, eliminating repeated permission prompts.

📋 Step 1: Initial Permission

When you first open the scanner, click "ALLOW" when prompted for camera access.

🔒 Step 2: Make Permission Permanent

Chrome/Edge:

  • Click the 🔒 lock icon in the address bar
  • Set Camera to "Allow"
  • Refresh the page

Safari (iOS):

  • Go to Settings > Safari > Camera
  • Set to "Allow"
  • Or tap "AA" in address bar > Website Settings > Camera > Allow

✅ Step 3: Verify Setup

After setting permissions:

  • Refresh the app
  • Click "Scan Barcode"
  • Scanner should open instantly without prompts
💡 Pro Tip: Add Tindahan Ko to your home screen for the best native app experience!

🌐 Browser-Specific Notes

Chrome: Best performance, full PWA support

Safari: May require manual permission setup

Firefox: Good compatibility, check privacy settings

Edge: Similar to Chrome, excellent support

🔧 Troubleshooting

If you still see permission prompts:

  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Check if camera is blocked in browser settings
  • Try incognito/private mode first, then normal mode
  • Ensure HTTPS connection (required for camera access)
